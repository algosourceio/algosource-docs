## Phase 3: Consistent Contribution (Months 1-3)

<div className="bg-neutral-800/50 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg p-6 mb-8">

### Goal: Build a Contribution Habit

Move from one-time contributor to regular community member.

### Tasks

<Checklist>
<ChecklistItem>Choose 1-2 projects to focus on deeply</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Submit at least 1 PR per week</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Graduate from "good first issues" to regular issues</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Start reviewing other PRs (even without merge rights)</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Help answer questions from newer contributors</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Join the project's Discord/Slack/chat</ChecklistItem>
<ChecklistItem>Follow the project's development discussions</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

### Time Investment
**5-10 hours per week**

### Milestone
âœ“ Maintainers recognize your username
