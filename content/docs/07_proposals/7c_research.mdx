# Researching Existing Solutions

Show you've done the work.

## What to Research

### Similar Projects

- How do other projects handle this?
- What can you learn from them?
- What did they get right/wrong?

### Existing Code

- Is there partial implementation already?
- What's been tried before?
- Why was it abandoned/paused?

### Related Issues

- What discussions exist?
- What are people asking for?
- What's blocking progress?

### Technical Solutions

- What technologies could you use?
- What are trade-offs?
- What's the standard approach?

## How to Present Research

In your proposal:

```markdown
## Related Work & Solutions

### Existing Approach
The current [X] uses [Y] to solve this.

### Limitations
However, [limitation 1] and [limitation 2] prevent it from [goal].

### My Approach
I will [your solution] because [reasoning].

### Advantages
This is better because [advantage 1], [advantage 2].
```

> **Research isn't busywork.** It proves you're serious and capable.
