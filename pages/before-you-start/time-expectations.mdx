## Communication Time

More time than you expect goes to communication:

| Activity | Time | Frequency |
|----------|------|-----------|
| Writing detailed PR descriptions | 30-60 min | Every PR |
| Responding to review comments | 15-30 min | Multiple times |
| Asking clarifying questions | 15-30 min | As needed |
| Participating in issue discussions | 30-60 min | Weekly |
| Reading project announcements | 15-30 min | Weekly |
| Community chat (Discord/Slack) | 30-60 min | Weekly |

## Time Management Strategies

<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-4">
<div className="bg-neutral-800/50 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg p-4">
<h4 className="font-semibold text-emerald-400 mb-2">Batch Your Work</h4>
<p className="text-sm text-neutral-300">Set aside 2-3 hour blocks instead of 30-minute snippets. Deep work is more effective.</p>
</div>

<div className="bg-neutral-800/50 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg p-4">
<h4 className="font-semibold text-blue-400 mb-2">Use Dead Time</h4>
<p className="text-sm text-neutral-300">Code review, issue reading, and documentation can be done on mobile during commutes.</p>
</div>

<div className="bg-neutral-800/50 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg p-4">
<h4 className="font-semibold text-purple-400 mb-2">Accept Wait Times</h4>
<p className="text-sm text-neutral-300">Don't let waiting block you. Work on multiple PRs or projects in parallel.</p>
</div>

<div className="bg-neutral-800/50 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg p-4">
<h4 className="font-semibold text-amber-400 mb-2">Track Your Time</h4>
<p className="text-sm text-neutral-300">For the first month, track how you actually spend time. You'll be surprised.</p>
</div>
